Week Two of 2016's Co-creation Sessions

Saturday 18 June 2016

In this week, teams who had nailed down their conceptual frameworks were doubling down on experiments in making and technology.

Some teams were still unclear on direction, with the program organizers working to help unblock and get the teams started.

As is the general pattern at this stage of the process, there were collaborations, breakthroughs and excitement, at the same time as exasperation, frustration and difficulties. However, as the day progressed, the teams were able to come together to push forward.

Over the coming weeks, the teams will take the direction they have established and end up with results to showcase. However at this stage the endgame is still unwritten for most teams.
