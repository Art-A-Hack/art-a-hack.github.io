About us

For over three years Art-A-Hack™ has supported teams from a variety of disciplines including art, technology, hardware and software development, design, immersive environments, music, theater, animation, social justice and interactivity.

Art-A-Hack™ matches participants selected from an open call according to skill level, interests and concept. Teams are provided with equipment, facilitation and collaborative workspaces in New York City.

Each iteration of the program then culminates in showcases and promotion opportunities for our teams. To learn more about our teams and their work, visit the alumni page and browse the project write-ups.

Leadership team

The Art-A-Hack™ program is managed and directed by founders Andrew McWilliams and Ellen Pearlman. This is a partnership between the ThoughtWorks and the Volumetric Society, two organizations committed to exploring creativity and technology.
