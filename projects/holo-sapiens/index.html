Holo Sapiens

A team from Art-A-Hack™ 2018 Special Edition — Cyfest, St. Petersburg, Russia

Team members:

  • Kseniia Galkina
  • Leonid Gavrilov
  • Mitya Akhmediyev

The Holo Sapiens team explored the relationship between text, speech, interactivity and illusion, resulting in the projection of an interactive holographic-like image.

Using Google APIs, the team explored text-to-speech input that triggered a classically represented Greek sculpture to move and perform.

The piece went on to be exhibited at the Tate Museum’s Tate Exchange program in London.

The piece was also exhibited at the QuartaRiata Artist Residency in St Petersburg in 2018.
